# Executive Summary

## Key Findings

### Regional Performance
- West region generated the highest sales.
- South region generated the lowest sales.

### Category Performance
- Technology generated the highest revenue.
- Office Supplies generated the lowest revenue.

### Profitability
- Technology generated the highest profit.
- Furniture generated the lowest profit.

### Recommendations
1. Increase focus on Technology products.
2. Review discount strategies for low-profit categories.
3. Investigate underperforming regions.
4. Promote high-margin products.

## Key Business Findings

### Regional Performance
- West generated the highest sales and profit.
- South generated the lowest sales.

### Category Performance
- Technology generated the highest sales and profit.
- Furniture generated strong sales but weak profitability.

### Product Analysis
- Phones were the highest-selling sub-category.
- Tables generated significant losses despite strong sales.

### Discount Impact
- Profitability declines significantly when discounts exceed 20%.

### Customer Segments
- Consumer customers generated the highest sales and profit.
